Military Life

I come from a family with a History of Military Service. I spent a little over 6 years in the Infantry, creating some of the best and worst experiences of my life, and will always cherish the relationships I forged with my squad members.

Paradise

I have always held a connection to the ocean. When I was young, we lived on the island of Guam because my dad was stationed at Anderson AFB there. In my own adult life I had the opportunity to be stationed in Hawaii for three years. I love the crashing waves, the vibrant colors, and the melodic peace you feel as you listen to nature's music.

Man's Best Friend

Dogs bring joy to every moment in Life. Throughout my life I have had the opportunity to call several dogs my best friend. This is Tanner, my childhood dog and my first furry friend. He had the kindest sould and the warmest heart. We would take him to the coast of Oregon to place called Pacific City, where he would run along the beach, climb the sand dunes, and the cold wind of the Pacific Northwest would ruffle that beautiful collie coat of his.
